## 2. What Orbit Is (Elevator Pitch)

Orbit is an AI-powered platform that unifies competitive intelligence, marketing planning, and product management — giving go-to-market teams the clarity to compete, plan, and build with confidence. Most tools give you data. Orbit gives you a complete system — from understanding your market to planning your response to building what wins.

---

## 3. The Problem Orbit Solves

Go-to-market teams operate across fragmented tools and disconnected workflows. Competitive intelligence lives in spreadsheets. Marketing plans live in slide decks. Product roadmaps live in project management tools. The result:

- **Insight gaps:** Teams don't know when competitors change positioning, launch features, or shift messaging until it's too late.
- **Slow response:** Translating a competitive insight into a marketing action or product decision takes weeks, not hours.
- **Silos:** Sales needs battlecards. Marketing needs messaging frameworks. Product needs gap analysis. Each team builds their own version from scratch.
- **Stale data:** Intelligence gathered last quarter drives decisions made this quarter.

Orbit eliminates these gaps by connecting the entire insight-to-action lifecycle in one platform.

## 4. The Three Pillars of GTM Excellence

### Pillar 1: Competitive Intelligence — *"Know Your Battlefield"*
Automated monitoring and AI-powered analysis of competitor positioning, messaging, and market movements.

**Key capabilities:**
- Automated website crawling and change detection
- AI-synthesized competitive analysis and gap identification
- News monitoring across competitor landscape
- Intelligence Briefings — periodic AI-generated market intelligence reports with executive summaries and action items
- Podcast-style audio briefings (two-host conversational format) for consuming intelligence on the go
- Intelligence Health dashboard showing data freshness across all sources and artifacts

### Pillar 2: Marketing Planner — *"Plan with Precision"*
Transform competitive insights into actionable marketing plans with AI-suggested activities, social posts, and email campaigns.

**Key capabilities:**
- GTM Plan generation grounded in competitive reality
- Messaging Framework development aligned to market positioning
- Campaign management with multi-channel social post generation (LinkedIn, X, Facebook)
- Email newsletter creation for Outlook, HubSpot, and Dynamics 365
- Content Library and Brand Library for centralized asset management
- Persona & ICP Builder with AI-assisted generation and CSV export

### Pillar 3: Product Management — *"Build What Matters"*
Align product development with market reality by tracking competitive features and prioritizing based on intelligence.

**Key capabilities:**
- Feature catalog and quarterly roadmap views
- AI-powered roadmap recommendations grounded in competitive gaps
- Product benchmarking against competitor offerings
- Product One Sheets for sales enablement
