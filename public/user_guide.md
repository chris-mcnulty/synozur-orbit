# Orbit User Guide

Welcome to Orbit, the AI-powered competitive intelligence platform by The Synozur Alliance. This guide will help you get the most out of your Orbit experience.

---

## Getting Started

### Setting Up Your Company Profile

After signing in for the first time, you'll be prompted to set up your company profile. This is your baseline for competitive analysis.

1. **Company Website**: Enter your main website URL
2. **Positioning Documents**: Upload any messaging guides, pitch decks, or brand documents
3. **Products**: Add your key products or services for product-level analysis

Your company profile serves as the foundation that Orbit uses to compare your positioning against competitors.

---

## Core Features

### Adding Competitors

Navigate to **Competitors** in the sidebar to add and manage your competitive landscape.

1. Click **Add Competitor**
2. Enter the competitor's website URL
3. Orbit will automatically crawl their website and extract key information

**What Orbit Captures:**
- Homepage messaging and value propositions
- About page content and company positioning
- Product/service descriptions
- Blog posts and thought leadership
- Social media links

### Running AI Analysis

The **Analysis** section is where Orbit's AI engine helps you understand your competitive position.

**Analysis Types:**
- **Quick Analysis**: Fast overview of messaging alignment (uses less AI credits)
- **Full Analysis**: Comprehensive competitive review with detailed recommendations
- **Full + Change Detection**: Includes monitoring for website changes since last analysis

**Analysis Outputs:**
- Key themes and messaging patterns
- Competitive gaps and opportunities
- Positioning recommendations
- Actionable next steps

### Viewing Recommendations

The **Recommendations** section consolidates all AI-generated insights across your analyses. Here you can:

- Review all recommendations in one place
- Mark recommendations as completed or dismissed
- Generate detailed long-form recommendations for specific items
- Track progress on implementing changes

## Advanced Features

### Documents & Grounding

Upload positioning documents to provide Orbit's AI with deeper context about your company:

- **Supported formats**: PDF, DOCX
- **Use cases**: Brand guidelines, messaging frameworks, sales playbooks, competitive battle cards

Documents you upload are used to ground AI analysis, ensuring recommendations align with your existing strategy.

### Assessments

Create point-in-time snapshots of your competitive landscape:

1. Navigate to **Assessments**
2. Click **New Assessment**
3. Select competitors to include
4. Run the assessment to capture current positioning

Assessments are useful for quarterly reviews, board presentations, or tracking progress over time.

### Projects

For consulting firms and agencies, **Projects** enables proxy analysis:

- Create projects for different clients
- Run company vs. company analysis
- Generate product-level battle cards
- Scope reports to specific projects

### Reports

Generate branded PDF reports to share with stakeholders:

1. Go to **Reports**
2. Select scope (full baseline or specific project)
3. Click **Generate Report**
4. Download the branded PDF

Reports include your company profile, competitor overview, analysis findings, and recommendations.

## Website Monitoring

Orbit can automatically monitor competitor websites for changes.

**How it works:**
1. Orbit crawls competitor websites on a schedule
2. AI compares new content against previous versions
3. You receive summaries of what changed

**Premium Feature**: Website monitoring is available on Pro and Enterprise plans.

## User Management

Admins can manage team access from **User Management**:

- **Invite team members** via email
- **Assign roles**: Standard User, Domain Admin, or Consultant
- **Manage permissions** based on role

**Role Hierarchy:**
- **Global Admin**: Full platform access across all tenants
- **Domain Admin**: Manage users and settings for your organization
- **Standard User**: Access to intelligence features
- **Consultant**: Cross-tenant read access for agency users

---

## Settings

Configure your account and organization settings:

- **Profile**: Update your name, email, and preferences
- **Organization**: Manage company details and branding
- **Billing**: View plan details and usage limits
- **Integrations**: Connect external services (Microsoft Entra SSO, etc.)

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Keep your company profile current** - Update positioning documents as your messaging evolves
2. **Add relevant competitors** - Focus on direct competitors for the most actionable insights
3. **Run regular analyses** - Monthly or quarterly analysis helps track competitive shifts
4. **Use grounding documents** - The more context you provide, the better Orbit's recommendations
5. **Share reports with stakeholders** - Keep your team aligned on competitive positioning
